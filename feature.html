About Innovator

At the heart of our innovative journey is Ashwini Kanhaiya Patil
, a hardworking and passionate student dedicated to making a difference. With an unwavering commitment to sustainability and a keen understanding of consumer needs, Ashwini has been working on this project for years, meticulously surveying people to uncover the perfect solution for an everyday necessity: pure, sustainable drinking water. Ashwini's vision is rooted in creating a world where convenience and environmental consciousness coexist seamlessly. By introducing mineral water in eco-friendly tetra packs, she aims to reduce plastic waste while maintaining the highest standards of purity and trust.

Say goodbye to single-uselessness. Say hello to grab and go, and go and go.

Each long-lasting, refillable EcoHydrate bottle replaces dozens, if not hundreds of single-use plastic bottles. It’s a great way to begin the end of single-use insanity.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the drinking water industry by offering sustainable and innovative packaging solutions that prioritize environmental health, consumer trust, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To inspire a global shift towards eco-conscious living by redefining the way we consume everyday essentials. Through dedication, innovation, and collaboration, we aim to lead the way in creating a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future.
