The STED Incubation Forum is an initiative designed to support student entrepreneurs by providing a comprehensive ecosystem within academic settings. STED focuses on innovation, entrepreneurship, and student support, aiming to nurture young innovators and aspiring entrepreneurs. By leveraging partnerships with government and non-government organizations, STED offers resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Its mission is to empower student entrepreneurs to transform their ideas into successful ventures, contributing to economic growth and social development, with a vision of fostering a vibrant entrepreneurial culture.
